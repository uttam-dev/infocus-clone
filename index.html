Two Web promotional movies "At Home" and "At a Restaurant" of Belgian Wheat Beer "Hoegaarden" were released. The theme is "IT'S APERO TIME: A delicious time that fills your heart with joy.", and the movie depicts how people go to a restaurant after work from their office or home, or to a friend's house on a day off, to enjoy a delicious time, freeing their minds by toasting with Hoegaarden.

IN FOCUS was in charge of direction and production.
